Bowl Types

1. Classic Açaí Bowl Description: The perfect mix of refreshing flavors for a timeless favorite. Base: Açaí, banana, mixed berries, almond milk Toppings: Granola, banana slices, strawberries, blueberries, coconut flakes, and a drizzle of honey




2. Tropical Paradise Bowl Description: A taste of the tropics with a fresh, fruity twist. Base: Açaí, pineapple, mango, coconut water Toppings: Fresh mango, kiwi slices, pineapple, coconut flakes, chia seeds, and a honey drizzle




3. Protein Power Bowl Description: Packed with protein and nutrients to fuel your day. Base: Açaí, banana, almond butter, almond milk, a scoop of plant-based protein Toppings: Granola, sliced almonds, banana, blueberries, hemp seeds, and a swirl of peanut butter




4. Berry Bliss Bowl Description: A berry lover’s dream with all the antioxidants. Base: Açaí, blueberries, raspberries, strawberries, almond milk Toppings: Blackberries, strawberries, blueberries, granola, cacao nibs, and a sprinkle of flax seeds




5. Green Goddess Bowl Description: Energizing greens blended with the classic açaí taste for a refreshing twist. Base: Açaí, spinach, kale, banana, coconut water Toppings: Kiwi, banana slices, granola, pumpkin seeds, coconut flakes, and a drizzle of agave
